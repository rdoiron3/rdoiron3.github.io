Education

Data Analytics

Post graduate diploma, NAIT class of 2023-2024

The Data analytics course at NAIT is a one year post graduate program focused Python, SQL, and Tableau. In addition to coding, the course also explores concepts such as data governance, business use cases, meaningful reporting, and efficient workflows. Like all of NAITs programs, the focus is on practicality and meeting the requirements of local firms.

The course is capstoned with a four month project where we acted as consultants for a real local company, analyzing their raw data from the customer service app and organizing it into meaningful findings and recommendations.
